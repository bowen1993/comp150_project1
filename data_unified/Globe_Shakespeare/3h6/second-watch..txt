What, will he not to bed?
'Tis the more honour, because more dangerous.
Ay, wherefore else guard we his royal tent, But to defend his person from night-foes?
Stay, or thou diest! The drum playing and trumpet sounding,  re-enter WARWICK, SOMERSET, and the rest,
bringing the KING out in his gown, sitting
in a chair. RICHARD and HASTINGS fly
over the stage.
