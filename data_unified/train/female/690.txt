I am paid for't now. Lead me from hence: I faint: O Iras, Charmian ! 'tis no matter. Go to the fellow, good Alexas; bid him Report the feature of Octavia, her years, Her inclination, let him not leave out The color of her hair: bring me word quickly. Let him for ever go :let him notCharmian, Though he be painted one way like a Gorgon, The other way's a Mars. Bid you Alexas To Mardian.  Bring me word how tall she is. Pity me, Charmian, But do not speak to me. Lead me to my chamber. Exeunt.
