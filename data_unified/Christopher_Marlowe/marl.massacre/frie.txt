My Lord, I come to bring you newes, that your brother the Cardinall of Loraine by the Kings consent is 
lately strangled unto death.
My Lord, heare me but speak  .  ,   I am a Frier of the order of the Jacobyns, that for my conscience sake will kill the King.
O my Lord, I have beene a great sinner in my dayes, and the deed is meritorious.
