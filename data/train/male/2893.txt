Alexas did revolt; and went to Jewry on Affairs of Antony; there did persuade Great Herod to incline himself to Csar, And leave his master Antony: for this pains Csar hath hang'd him. Canidius and the rest That fell away have entertainment, but No honorable trust. I have done ill; Of which I do accuse myself so sorely, That I will joy no more.
