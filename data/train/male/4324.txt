'Imprimis, It is agreed between the French king Charles, and William de la Pole, Marquess of Suffolk, ambassador for Henry King of England, that the said Henry shall espouse the Lady Margaret, daughter unto Reignier-King of Naples, Sicilia and Jerusalem, and crown her Queen of England ere the thirtieth day of May next ensuing. Item, that the duchy of Anjou and the county of Maine shall be released and delivered to the king her father'--
